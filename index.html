Product Range — All PVC Pipes

We manufacture a complete range of PVC pipes for multiple applications. Below are our core categories.

PVC-U (Pressure) Pipes

For cold water plumbing & distribution. Available in sizes 20mm–315mm and various pressure ratings.

Drainage & Sewer Pipes

Robust pipes for drainage, sewage and stormwater applications with smooth bore for optimal flow.

Agricultural & Irrigation Pipes

Lightweight and cost-effective pipes suitable for drip, sprinkler and bulk irrigation systems.

Fittings & Accessories

Elbows, tees, couplers and end caps — tested and compatible for leak-free joints.
